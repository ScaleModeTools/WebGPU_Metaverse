# Weapon balance starting points

These are **authoring starting points**, not final balance numbers.

## Core philosophy

- pistol = responsive sidearm
- SMG = forgiving close-range bullet hose
- battle rifle = burst precision mid-range anchor
- shotgun = close-range deletion with spread control attachments
- sniper = long-range precision with staged zoom
- rocket launcher = slow, heavy, punishing ordnance with lock-on support

## What modules are supposed to do

### Grip

Changes:

- recoil
- movement bloom
- ADS transition
- sometimes move-speed multiplier

### Front / rear sight

Changes:

- ADS spread
- acquisition feel
- sometimes slight hip-fire help

### Optic

Changes:

- zoom levels
- ADS FOV
- reticle presentation
- usually a little precision/range help
- often a small ADS-speed tradeoff

### Muzzle

Changes:

- recoil
- range
- spread
- projectile guidance (launcher)
- pellet spread (shotgun)

## Progression pattern in this bundle

- starter: pistol + simple irons
- early unlocks: SMG + basic optics
- mid unlocks: battle rifle + shotgun + control attachments
- late unlocks: sniper + launcher + challenge-driven heavy modules

## Things to tune after first playtest

- battle rifle burst cadence and recovery
- shotgun pellet spread versus one-shot range
- launcher splash and self-damage radius
- ADS transition timing across heavy weapons
- optic power relative to map scale
